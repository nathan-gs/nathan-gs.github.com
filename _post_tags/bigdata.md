Big Data refers to the technologies and practices for processing, storing, and analyzing datasets too large or complex for traditional tools. From distributed computing frameworks to real-time streaming architectures, Big Data enables organizations to derive insights from massive volumes of structured and unstructured data.

- [Kappa Architecture](/tags/kappa-architecture) — Stream-first data architectures
- [Data Mesh](/tags/data-mesh) — Decentralized, domain-oriented data ownership
